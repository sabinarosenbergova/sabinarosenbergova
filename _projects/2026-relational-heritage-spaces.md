This participatory workshop, organised as part of the Netherlands Chapter of the Association of Critical Heritage Studies, brought together scholars and practitioners to explore the concept of relational heritage spaces. Held at the Courtroom of the Faculty of Religion, Culture and Society at the University of Groningen, the full-day event (10–17h) featured keynote lectures by Hester Dibbits (Reinwardt Academy) and Cindy Zalm (Wereldmuseum Amsterdam), followed by group discussions on critical approaches to heritage.

The workshop marked a first step toward forming a Netherlands Chapter of the Association of Critical Heritage Studies, fostering dialogue across disciplines on how heritage spaces are shaped by and shape social, cultural, and political relations.
