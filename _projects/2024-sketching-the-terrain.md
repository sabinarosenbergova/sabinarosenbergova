The Groningen Heritage Network, in partnership with the Rudolf Agricola School's Environmental Heritage Research Group, hosted this symposium focused on diverse perspectives on heritage.

The event brought together experts from various disciplines, including archaeology, spatial sciences, and environmental studies, to explore the intersection of heritage and sustainability.

The afternoon began with a dynamic panel discussion, where speakers from different fields shared their insights on how heritage can inform contemporary challenges. Participants then broke into smaller discussion groups, fostering deeper conversations and collaborative problem-solving. A plenary session followed, allowing for a collective reflection on the key themes of the day.
